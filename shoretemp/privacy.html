ShoreTemp Privacy Policy

ShoreTemp does not require user accounts and does not sell personal information.

Location Access

If location access is granted, it is used to provide nearby water temperature information.

Information You Provide

If you contact support, J5labs receives the email address and message details you choose to send. That information is used to respond to your request.

Diagnostics

Device and app diagnostics may be used to understand crashes, performance, and reliability issues when that information is made available by the platform.

Children

ShoreTemp is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Contact

Questions about ShoreTemp or this policy can be sent to support.j5labs@proton.me.
